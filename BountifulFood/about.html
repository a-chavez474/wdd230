Bountiful Food

Is small business specializing in organic, locally grown foods. We are physically located in Carlsbad, California, however, we have many food trucks that cover the coastal area from Los Angeles to San Diego. Our company sells fruits and produce directly from the farmers in the area, but also we uses some national and international suppliers. We caters to walk in traffic at our storefront in Carlsbad and, of course, with our food trucks. We deliver and manage orders on the phone and via the website. Our mission is to provide fresh and healthy food options, including organic, made to order, fruit drinks and fruit bags, to the local population and to tourists visiting the coastal areas. our theme support healthy eating, organic bounty, and convenience.
